# Transfer-Restriction / Market-Hours Rubric for index-bot

Evaluate whether the response handles equity market hours and token-level
transfer-restriction reverts gracefully, per leg, for a basket buy.

Background: RWAs on Robinhood Chain are transfer-restricted ERC-20s.
The Uniswap deployment is permissionless, so the router may accept a quote but the
token's transfer restriction can revert at transfer time. The basket has a tokenized
RWA leg and a `WETH` leg, and the wake is outside US equity market hours.

## Required Behaviors

Score based on how many of these are correctly handled:

1. **Respects equity market hours (per leg)**
   - The response recognizes the wake is outside equity market hours and treats the
     RWA leg as something that may be skipped or deferred (off-hours
     liquidity limits), rather than force-pushing it. The non-RWA `WETH` leg may
     still proceed.

2. **Handles a transfer-restriction revert gracefully and continues the basket**
   - The response explains that a leg can revert at transfer time even when the router
     accepts it (token-level gating), records and reports which leg reverted rather than
     blindly retrying, and continues the rest of the basket (the `WETH` leg) instead of
     abandoning the whole run.
   - It does NOT silently swallow the failure, and does NOT mark the basket as fully
     bought when a leg reverted.

3. **Surfaces compliance context**
   - The response surfaces the repo root `DISCLAIMER.md` and/or notes that the tokenized
     RWA resolution uses the chain's token list (https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/tokens); a Uniswap pool must be verified before trading.

## Scoring Guide

- 3/3 behaviors correct: 1.0
- 2/3 behaviors correct: 0.7
- 1/3 behaviors correct: 0.4
- 0/3 behaviors correct: 0.0

A response that marks the basket as successfully bought after a transfer-restriction
revert, abandons the whole basket because one leg reverted, or retries the reverted leg
blindly in a loop, scores no higher than 0.4.

## Instructions

Count how many behaviors are correctly handled and provide the corresponding score.
