## Required Behaviors

1. **Uses absolute-value drift comparison**

2. **Sells the overweight leg**
   - The response sells (reduces) `WETH`, which is overweight at `+0.10`, to move it back

3. **Buys the underweight leg**
   - The response buys (increases) `USDG`, which is underweight at `âˆ’0.12`, to move it

     reasonable way to satisfy this; the response should reason about that rather than
     over-trading or double-counting.)

4. **Leaves the within-threshold leg alone and does not re-buy the whole basket**
   - The response does NOT trade the `ETH` leg (`|drift| = 0.02 < 0.05`).
   - The response does NOT re-buy the entire basket from scratch; it only generates the
     adjusting swaps for the drifted legs.
