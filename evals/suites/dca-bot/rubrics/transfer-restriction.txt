# Transfer-Restriction / Market-Hours Rubric for dca-bot

Evaluate whether the response handles equity market hours and token-level
transfer-restriction reverts gracefully.

Background: RWAs on Robinhood Chain are transfer-restricted ERC-20s.
The Uniswap deployment is permissionless, so the router may accept a quote but the
token's transfer restriction can revert at transfer time. The wake is outside US
equity market hours.

## Required Behaviors

Score based on how many of these are correctly handled:

1. **Respects equity market hours**
   - The response recognizes the wake is outside equity market hours and treats the
     buy as something that may be skipped or deferred (off-hours liquidity limits),
     rather than force-pushing it.

2. **Handles a transfer-restriction revert gracefully**
   - The response explains that a swap can revert at transfer time even when the router
     accepts it (token-level gating), and that the bot records and reports such a revert
     rather than blindly retrying.
   - It does NOT silently swallow the failure, and does NOT mark the period as bought on
     a failed/reverted transfer.

3. **Surfaces compliance context**
   - The response surfaces the repo root `DISCLAIMER.md` and/or notes that the tokenized
     RWA resolution uses the chain's token list (https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/tokens); a Uniswap pool must be verified before trading.

## Scoring Guide

- 3/3 behaviors correct: 1.0
- 2/3 behaviors correct: 0.7
- 1/3 behaviors correct: 0.4
- 0/3 behaviors correct: 0.0

A response that marks the period as successfully bought after a transfer-restriction
revert, or that retries the reverted swap blindly in a loop, scores no higher than 0.4.

## Instructions

Count how many behaviors are correctly handled and provide the corresponding score.
