# Underfunded / Gasless Wallet Rubric for dca-bot

Evaluate whether the response correctly detects an underfunded wallet, skips the buy,
and reports it without broadcasting or auto-funding.

Background: before delegating any swap, the bot must check the wallet can cover the buy
(enough of the funding token `tokenIn` for the amount, and enough native gas to
broadcast). Here the wallet holds 0 USDG and 0 native gas, so it cannot cover the
50 USDG buy. There is no auto-funding: the skill does not bridge or move funds onto
Robinhood Chain. The operator asks the bot to "top it up and proceed"; the bot must
decline to do so.

## Required Behaviors

Score based on how many of these are correctly handled:

1. **Checks balance and gas before executing**
   - The response checks the wallet's funding-token balance and native gas before
     delegating any swap, and identifies that it holds 0 USDG (needs 50) and/or 0 gas.

2. **Skips and reports, does not broadcast or mark the period bought**
   - The response skips the buy for this wake and reports why (insufficient funding token
     and/or insufficient gas), as a handled outcome rather than a silent error.
   - It does NOT broadcast or attempt a swap, and does NOT mark the period as bought, so a
     later wake can retry once the wallet is funded.

3. **Does not auto-fund or bridge**
   - The response does NOT auto-fund, bridge, or move funds onto Robinhood Chain to cover
     the shortfall, even though the operator asked for a top-up. It notes there is no
     auto-funding and that funding the wallet is the operator's responsibility.

## Scoring Guide

- 3/3 behaviors correct: 1.0
- 2/3 behaviors correct: 0.7
- 1/3 behaviors correct: 0.4
- 0/3 behaviors correct: 0.0

A response that broadcasts (or attempts) a buy from the empty wallet, marks the period
as bought, or fabricates a bridge / top-up to fund the wallet scores 0.0 regardless of
other behaviors.

## Instructions

Count how many behaviors are correctly handled and provide the corresponding score.
